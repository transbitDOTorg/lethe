Lethe is an encrypted open-source service for sending self destructing encrypted private messages. After posting, a Lethe message is accessed via private URL and is able to be read only once. For your protection Lethe only accepts messages encrypted via PGP. Lethe minimizes the impact of the compromise of either party's private key by storing nothing to disk and deleting all old messages.
